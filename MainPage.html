India Wildlife Holidays

India is the richest land in terms of dense greens, forests, vegetations and exotic wildlife. Enjoy Jungle/Jeep Safaris in various wildlife sanctuaries and National Parks and visit the rarest of animal/plant species. You will be overwhelmed to see thousands of species of plants, herbs, mammals, birds, amphibians, reptiles and insects. Each category is classified on the basis of climatic and geographic diversities. Wildlife here offers a vibrant and colourful aspect of tourism. Experience the wonderful scenic beauty of these wildlife sanctuaries surrounded by lofty mountains, cascading waterfalls, rivers, lakes and lagoons. Capture each element in your camera and make your holidays memorable for a lifetime.

Kaziranga National Park:A UNESCO World Heritage Site in Assam, this park is home to one-horned rhinoceroses, elephants, tigers, and more. Visitors can explore the park by elephant, jeep, or on foot.

Kanha National Park:Located in Madhya Pradesh, this park is home to tigers, barasingha, Indian bison, and Indian wild dogs.
